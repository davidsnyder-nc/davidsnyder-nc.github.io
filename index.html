AI News Feed App

AI News Feed is a modern web application that generates in-depth, AI-powered news stories across 20+ categories. It leverages multiple AI models (Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT) and can fetch real news from APIs like GNews, NewsAPI.org, The Guardian, and NY Times. The app is designed for flexibility, customizability, and ease of use.

Key Features

  • Generate news stories in 20+ categories (Tech, Science, Movies, etc.)
  • Choose from multiple AI models: Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT
  • Integrate with real news APIs for up-to-date content
  • Text-to-speech audio generation (Google Cloud TTS)
  • Custom story input and local weather integration
  • Copy to clipboard and NotebookLM integration
  • Modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface
